About Me

Frequently Asked

I believe great design is invisible — it solves problems elegantly without drawing attention to itself. I focus on clarity, accessibility, and creating delightful moments that build trust with users over time.

I've designed for fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, and SaaS products. I love learning new domains and translating complex requirements into simple, elegant experiences that feel obvious in hindsight.

Led design for a 0-to-1 product that reached 1M users in year one. Built a design system adopted across 5 product teams. Mentored junior designers and established design practices at two startups.

I believe the best work happens through partnership. I actively involve engineers early, align closely with PMs on goals before touching pixels, and always advocate for the user in every decision.
